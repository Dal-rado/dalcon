ABOUT US

An About Us page isn't just where you share the story of your brand. It's also where you tell your customer what you do for them and how you work to meet their needs in that area. It isn't so much “Here's what we're about,” but more like “Here's who we are, why we started, and what we can do for you.”3 Jan 2023

We consider ourselves to be able to offer professional expertise in a proactive, non-contractual approach which will contribute to the success of each project from an early stage, this is demonstrated by the repeat business we receive from satisfied clients. We provide a service for the Central belt and West of Scotland. All operatives are directly employed and are provided with the relevant training. We believe the quality of our workforce is a major contributor to our success. We are an accredited contractor with The Contractors Health & Safety Assessment Scheme. Our engineers work with the latest surveying equipment and are able to produce 3D ground models to calculate volumes and can assist in developing the most efficient cut and fill for any job. We own the majority of our own plant ranging from 20t excavators to 1t excavators, 9t dumpers to 1t dumpers and all small plant which can be supplemented with additional plant and equipment to suit our client’s needs
